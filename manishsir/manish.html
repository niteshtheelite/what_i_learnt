About Me

Dr. Kumar is a distinguished academician and accomplished professional in the field of Information Technology with a strong educational background and extensive experience in academia and research. Over the years, he has held key academic positions including Associate Professor at Regional College of Engineering and Principal at Vidya Vihar Institute of Technology, Bihar. A dedicated researcher, Dr. Kumar has numerous contributions to his field through papers and publications in esteemed National and International journals. He has also served participants in various seminars and conferences, presenting insightful research papers that have made significant contributions to the technological domain. His expertise spans diverse areas of IT engineering and management. Dr. Kumar is also an active member of various professional associations and societies such as the International Association of Engineers, the Computer Society of India, and the Institution of Engineers India, which underscores his commitment to continuous learning and professional development. Through his leadership in education and research, Currently Dr. Kumar serves as the Director at L.N Mishra Mishra College of Business Management, Muzaffarpur, where he continues to lead with vision, fostering academic excellence and innovation in management and technology education.
